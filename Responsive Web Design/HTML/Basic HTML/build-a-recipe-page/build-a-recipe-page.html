Cosmic cupcakes from the deep void

Summon a batch of spooky, starry treats inspired by ancient sea monsters, glowing galaxies, and mysterious tentacles.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Cocoa powder - 1/4 cup
  • Baking powder - 1 tsp
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Egg - 1
  • Butter, melted - 1/4 cup
  • Vanilla essence - a few drops
  • Gummy worms or liquorice strips - 1 bag for tenticles
  • Edible glitter or sprinkles - for cosmic dust

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and place cupcake cases in a tray.
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Add the milk, egg, melted butter, vanilla, and green food colouring.
  4. Stir until smooth and slightly monstrous.
  5. Spoon the mixture into cupcake cases.
  6. Bake for 15–18 minutes, or until the cakes spring back when touched.
  7. Let them cool completely.
  8. Add purple icing, tentacle decorations, and a sprinkle of edible glitter.

Serving Suggestion

Serve under dim lights with mysterious music and a warning sign:

“Do not stare too long into the frosting… it may stare back.”

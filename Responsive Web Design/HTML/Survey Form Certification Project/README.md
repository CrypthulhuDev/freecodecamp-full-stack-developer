## What I Built

### Jurassic Research Park Visitor Survey

I built a themed visitor survey form for a fictional dinosaur research park.

The form collects:

- Visitor contact information
- Favourite park attraction
- Suggestions for new park features
- Favourite food option
- Written visitor feedback
- Overall visit rating

## Reflection

This lab was good practice for building a complete HTML survey form from scratch. I worked with different input types, labels, fieldsets, legends, dropdown menus, checkboxes, radio buttons, textareas, and buttons.

I also practised organising form content into clear sections, which makes the page easier to read, use, and understand. The Jurassic Research Park theme made the lab more fun while still helping me practise important form elements and structure.
